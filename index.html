About

Reflux, the Annual Symposium organized by the Department of Chemical Engineering of IIT Guwahati, has been a pioneer in chemical Industrial and entrepreneurial scene of the country. The aim of the seventh edition of Reflux is to provide an opportunity for budding Chemical Engineers to share scientific expertise and knowledge towards the development of new methods and strategies in different fields of Chemical Engineering. The event is planned to be highly interactive and an excellent learning experience for all the participants. Reflux 7.0 offers several events including invited lectures, workshops and a wide range of exhibitions and competitions to be held during these three days along with research paper presentations. Over the years, Reflux has proved itself as a genuine platform to interact with the youth and engage them.
